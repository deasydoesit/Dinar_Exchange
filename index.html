The People's Crytpocurrency Exchange of South Africa

Dinar Card

The Dinar Card is a debit-style card that allows users to connect to their Dinar Exchange account in order to withdraw and spend their crypto-funds. The Dinar Card will intergrate with the Dinar ATMs to offer a seamless withdraw experience and with legacy merchant POS systems to provide for a conventional, but cryto-based, payment experience for users

Dinar Token

The Dinar Token is an Ethereum-based ERC20 token that can be used to pay exchange fees on the Dinar Exchange. Additionally, the Dinar Token will be tethered to the South African Rand through audited and transparent asset backing. In the future, it is planned that the Dinar Token will able to be redemed for Rand at one of our partner institutions.

Dinar ATM

Dinar ATMs will be distributed across South Africa and will enable users to buy and sell crytocurrencies offered on the Dinar Exchange such as Bitcoin, Ethereum, and the like for cash at automated teller machines. Also, after issuance and audit, the Dinar Token will be able to be bought and sold at the Dinar ATMs for South African Rand.
